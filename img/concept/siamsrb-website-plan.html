MUST-HAVE FEATURES

These features will separate Siams RB from every competitor in Zambia with a website.

Numbers that count up when scrolled to: years, projects, cities, clients. Adds dynamism and communicates experience instantly.

Before & after photos, cold room installs, AC units. Real photos = real proof. Filterable by service type using Bootstrap tabs.
📋
Quote Request Form Must Have
Simple form: Name, Phone, Service, City, Description. Sends email to info@siamsrb.com. The single most important conversion tool.
⭐
Testimonials Carousel Must Have
Bootstrap carousel with client testimonials. Social proof is the #1 trust signal for service businesses in Zambia.
📰
Blog / Tips Section Nice to Have
3–5 helpful articles targeting "AC repair Lusaka", "cold room installation Zambia" etc. Long-term SEO strategy.
🌙
Load-Shedding Tip Widget Nice to Have
A small banner or tooltip reminding visitors to protect appliances during load-shedding — unique to Zambia, builds relevance.
🏙️
City Pages Nice to Have
Separate landing pages for Lusaka, Ndola, Livingstone, Solwezi, Mufulira. Massive for local Google search rankings.
🔍
SEO Metadata Must Have
Every page needs a unique title tag, meta description, and OG image. Target: "refrigeration repair Lusaka", "AC installation Zambia".

Mobile-First Design Must Have
Majority of Zambia's internet users are on mobile. Bootstrap's grid handles this — but test on real phones before launching.

BEFORE YOU GO LIVE

Non-negotiables before uploading to cPanel.

Item Details Priority
Real Photos Get actual photos of completed projects, team members, equipment. No stock photos of white guys in hard hats. Critical
Google Business Profile Create/claim the Google Business listing for Siams RB. Links to Maps embed. Drives local search traffic. Critical
3–5 Testimonials Collect real client quotes. Name + company + service used. Even WhatsApp screenshots work as a start. Critical
Contact Form Works Test the quote form sends email to info@siamsrb.com. Use a PHP mailer or formspree.io on cPanel. Critical
SSL Certificate Enable free SSL via cPanel (Let's Encrypt). HTTPS is required for trust and Google ranking. Critical

Google Analytics Add GA4 tracking code to see where visitors come from and which pages convert. Recommended
